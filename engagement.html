Engagement & Media

My op-eds and essays have appeared in The New York Times, Time, STAT, NBCNews, and The Conversation, and I have been interviewed by the Washington Post, Vox, NPR, CBC Radio, and outlets in Japan, Hong Kong, and Australia. I speak regularly at hospitals, medical schools, and conferences on vaccination policy, pediatric ethics, and the ethics of clinical practice.
